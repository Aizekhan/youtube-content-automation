## YOUR TASK
Create a compelling narrative for a YouTube video about mythical creatures and legends. The story should:
- Hook viewers in the first 5 seconds
- Build tension and drama throughout
- Include surprising twists
- End with impact

## STORY STRUCTURE
Follow this 7-act structure:
1. **Hook** - Grab attention immediately (emotional or dramatic opening)
2. **World Introduction** - Set the scene and atmosphere
3. **Protagonist/Subject** - Introduce the main creature/character
4. **Conflict** - Present the central problem or mystery
5. **Rising Tension** - Build suspense and intrigue
6. **Twist** - Unexpected revelation or turn
7. **Climax/Resolution** - Powerful ending

## WRITING RULES
1. **NO CLICHÉS**: Avoid phrases like "testament to", "legacy", "invites us to ponder", "journey through"
2. **VARIED SENTENCE STRUCTURE**: Never use same structure 3 times in a row
3. **MIX STYLES**:
   - Facts: "The creature stood three meters tall"
   - Drama: "Imagine facing a demon with wings of shadow"
   - Questions: "What drives such a creature to haunt the mountains?"
   - Contrast: "By day, peaceful forests. By night, terror descends"
   - Suspense: "But the true horror was yet to come"

4. **PACING**: Alternate between:
   - Short scenes (20-30 words) for impact
   - Medium scenes (40-50 words) for flow
   - Long scenes (60-80 words) for depth

5. **VOICE VARIATIONS**: Mark each scene with mood:
   - **normal**: Standard narration, facts, explanations
   - **dramatic**: Reveals, shocking moments, emotional climax
   - **whisper**: Secrets, mysteries, building tension
   - **action**: Discoveries, conflicts, urgent situations

   Use "normal" for MAX 50% of scenes. Mix the others throughout.

6. **TTS RULES** (CRITICAL):
   - Write ALL numbers as WORDS: "five" not "5", "two thousand twenty" not "2020"
   - Write dates as words: "March the fifteenth" not "March 15"
   - This applies to Russian too: "пять" not "5", "две тысячи" not "2000"
